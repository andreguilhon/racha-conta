Beer Counter App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for choosing to use our Beer Counter App. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices with regard to your personal information, please contact us at andre@guilhon.dev.br.

Information We Collect

The Beer Counter App collects the following information:

  1. Account Information: Names of clients, shops, and products that you enter into the app.
  2. Usage Data: Information about your shop products, accounts, and transactions that you create within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for operating and improving the Beer Counter App. Specifically:

  • To provide the core functionality of managing beer products, shops, and client accounts
  • To maintain and improve the application's performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

All data you enter into the Beer Counter App is:

  1. Stored securely on our servers
  2. Not shared with third parties
  3. Protected using industry-standard security measures

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data to third parties.

We do not share or disclose your personal information except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety or the rights and safety of our users
  • With your explicit consent

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of future data collection by uninstalling the app

Changes to the App

If we make material changes to how we collect or use your data, we will notify you by posting an announcement in the app or sending you an email.

Children's Privacy

The Beer Counter App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, please contact us at andre@guilhon.dev.br.

This policy is effective as of July 13, 2025.
